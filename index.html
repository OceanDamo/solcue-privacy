Overview

SolCue is a circadian rhythm optimization app that helps users track outdoor light exposure. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your tracking data remains on your device only.

Local Storage

SolCue stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Daily light exposure tracking times
  • Session duration records
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Location Services

SolCue may request access to your location to:

  • Calculate accurate sunrise and sunset times for your area
  • Provide personalized circadian rhythm guidance

Location data is used only for real-time calculations and is not stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

SolCue uses the sunrise-sunset.org API to obtain sunrise and sunset times. This service receives only your approximate location coordinates (no personal information) to provide accurate solar data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your privacy is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

Children's Privacy

SolCue does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
